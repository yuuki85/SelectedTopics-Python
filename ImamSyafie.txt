Quotes of Imam Syafie

“True knowledge is that which benefits oneself or others, not information that is merely memorised.” 

“Don’t love the one who doesn’t love Allah. If they can leave Allah, they will leave you.” 

“You will never be able to please everyone,rather rectify what is between you and Allah and do not care about the people.” 

“Knowledge is not measured by how much is memorized, but rather by how much it is acted on” 

“The more I learn; the more I learn of my ignorance.”

“If you focused all your efforts into pleasing people, you’d never succeed, so be sincere to Allaah in your actions and intentions.”

“Nobility has four pillars: good character, generosity, humility, and piety”
